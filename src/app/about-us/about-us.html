OUR STORY

THE KAYSONS GROUP JOURNEY

Honda is globally recognized as the world’s largest manufacturer of two-wheelers, and Kaysons Honda has proudly carried forward this legacy since its establishment in 1997. Over the years, Kaysons Honda has earned a strong reputation as a trusted Honda dealership for scooters and motorcycles, known for delivering excellent sales, repair, and service experiences. Serving customers from Siliguri, North Bengal, Sikkim, Dooars, Bhutan, and nearby regions, the dealership has become a preferred destination for quality automobiles and dependable customer support.

The foundation of the Kaysons Group was built through dedication, hard work, and a vision for growth. Mr. Krishan K. Agarwal, the main promoter, began his journey by assisting his father in commission and consignee operations while continuing his studies. With growing goodwill and business experience, the family expanded into transportation by operating a fleet of Tata trucks. Recognizing the increasing demand for automobile replacement and spare parts, Mr. Agarwal established “Nirmal Auto Parts,” which soon became one of the leading automobile spare parts businesses across North Bengal, Sikkim, Dooars, Bhutan, and surrounding upcountry markets.

Today, the Kaysons Group is managed by Mr. Krishan K. Agarwal along with his three sons, who collectively oversee the group’s growing business operations. The group consists of multiple successful companies including Kaysons Automotive Private Limited, Kaysons Infrastructure Private Limited, Kaysons Ventures Private Limited, Nirmal Auto Parts, Nirmal Raj Service Station, and Anusiya Tie Up Private Limited.
