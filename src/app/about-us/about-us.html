OUR STORY

THE KAYSONS GROUP JOURNEY

Kaysons Honda is part of the well-established KAYSONS Group, a trusted business house with deep roots in the automotive industry. The journey began with the father of Mr. Krishan K. Agarwal, who worked as a commission and consignee agent. While continuing his education, Mr. Agarwal actively supported the business and helped build a strong reputation based on trust, dedication, and customer service.

As the business grew, the family entered the transportation sector with a single second-hand truck. Over time, this expanded into a fleet of Tata trucks, serving the growing needs of the region. Recognizing the increasing demand for quality automotive components, Mr. Krishan K. Agarwal established Nirmal Auto Parts. Today, it is one of the leading automobile spare parts businesses across North Bengal, Sikkim, the Dooars region, Bhutan, and surrounding upcountry markets.

Supported by authorized partnerships with leading brands such as Tata Motors, Tata Timken, Lucas TVS, Rane, SRMT, Purolator, and others, Nirmal Auto Parts has earned a respected position in the automotive trade. Under the leadership of Mr. Krishan K. Agarwal and his three sons, the KAYSONS Group has expanded into a diversified group of companies that includes Kaysons Automotive Private Limited, Kaysons Infrastructure Private Limited, Kaysons Ventures Private Limited, Nirmal Auto Parts, Nirmal Raj Service Station, and Ansuiya Tie Up Private Limited.

Kaysons Automotive Private Limited is an ISO 9001 and ISO 14001 certified company. Over the years, the group has represented renowned automobile brands including General Motors, LML, Nissan, Hyundai, Tata Motors, Kia, Honda Motorcycle & Scooter India, and Honda Premium Motorcycles.

The group has also expanded into hospitality with The Four Vedas, a luxury hotel and resort near Siliguri. At Kaysons Honda, customer satisfaction remains at the heart of everything we do. With a dedicated team of long-serving employees and a strong commitment to community welfare through the Gopiram Ginadevi Baporia Charitable Trust, we continue to build lasting relationships based on trust, service, and excellence.
